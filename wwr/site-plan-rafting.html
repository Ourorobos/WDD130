Overview

Purpose

1. Boost sales of company. 2. Help pepole find infomation on our comapny, company services (rafting trips), and white water rafting in general. 3. Make it easier for pepole to book a time/trip.

Audience

Our target audience are normal pepole of decent income looking for a fun time on there weekend or vacation. It is expected that our audience will mostly be using a phone primarily and a computer secondly. It is also expected that they will be looking for inforamtion about the trip. Where is it? (Also info unique to the route like class and amout of rapids) When is it? What will be provided, like Flotation devices and helmets. What is recomened to bring, like shoes that drain quickly.
